Welcome to Wizard's Tower!

You are a thief infiltrating the lost tower of Araxus the Arcane in search of valuable magical artefacts. As you explore, you will solve puzzles, translate Araxus' coded notes, and discover what he was like and why he disappeared.

This game uses your browser's local storage. You can choose to reset the game and delete all storage data related to the game with the 'reset game' option in the sidebar.

Without further ado, let's get started!
