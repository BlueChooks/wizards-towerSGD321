You fill the bowl with catnip, sprinkle on some sleep potion, and step back.

After a moment, you hear a snuffling, and a horrifically mutated cat the size of a horse appears from behind a group of crates. It doesn't seem to notice you as it stalks toward the bowl.

It sniffs the bowl for a moment, then starts to nibble on the catnip. After a few seconds of enthusiastic munching, the creature's head starts nodding. It sways on its feet, its eyes drift closed, then it collapses in a snoring heap.

The path to the ladder is clear.
