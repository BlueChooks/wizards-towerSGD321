The grubby letter is creased and covered with footprints. You brush it off and read:

Dear Mr Araxus,

The Wizard's Guild has received word of your great magical talent, and we would like to extend an invitation to you to join our ranks. We believe you have much to offer the Guild, and, should you be so inclined, we believe you would be a suitable candidate for a position of leadership.

We eagerly await your response.

Yours sincerely,

Morris White (Secretary to Archwizard Wintergrist)
